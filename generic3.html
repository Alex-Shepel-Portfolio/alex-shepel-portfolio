Runner with snake mechanics

What was implemented in this project:

1. Movement of the character along a pre-created route. The movement goes from point to point with an allowable distance from a point to point, this allows free movement in the lateral direction and does not create an unrestricted player.

2. Picking and adding parts of the tail to the player's body. Also setting the logic for removing and shifting these parts.

3. Destructible blocks after making a certain number of parts of the Snake and blocks of destruction.

4. Departure and accumulation of points when picking up parts of the tail.

A completed system for following a given route with the possibility of freedom of movement on the sides

Adding and Deleting Tail Parts. It took 2 days to implement the mechanics
