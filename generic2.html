Crowd Mehanic

What was implemented in this project:

1. Crowd control through the player

2. Automatic aiming at enemies and shooting

3. The weapon script is almost the same as in "Voxel Destroyer", but with additions. Shooting goes through the reads of the attacked area and there is inflicted damage.

4. The crowd is kept in a certain radius that adjusts to the number of objects in the crowd.

Automatic targeting of enemies

The crowd keeps within its radius, which removes the chance of losing one of them.
